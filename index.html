How to play

  1. Select a cat from the list on the left
  2. Click on cat image to increase the click count
  3. Click on another cat to change its click count
  4. Click on the admin panel if you want to change the cat name, image, or count
    • You can use an image from the internet or your computer
    • To get the address for a picture, open the picture in another tab of your browser to get the path
  5. Click Submit to change or click Cancel to cancel changes
